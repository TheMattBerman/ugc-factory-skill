# Scene Settings

Quick reference for UGC scene settings and their prompt elements.

---

## Home Settings

| Setting | Prompt Elements | Best For |
|---------|-----------------|----------|
| **Kitchen Counter** | bright modern kitchen, marble countertop, natural window light | Food, beverages, health, morning routines |
| **Bathroom Mirror** | clean bathroom, mirror selfie angle, soft lighting | Skincare, beauty, personal care |
| **Living Room Couch** | cozy living room, comfortable seating, casual | General testimonials, relaxed reviews |
| **Home Office** | desk setup, computer visible, organized space | Tech, productivity, remote work |
| **Bedroom** | neat bedroom, natural light, personal space | Fashion, morning/night routines |

## On-the-Go Settings

| Setting | Prompt Elements | Best For |
|---------|-----------------|----------|
| **Car Interior** | car front seat, natural daylight, steering wheel visible | Commute products, busy lifestyle |
| **Walking Outside** | outdoor walking, urban background, natural light | Fitness, lifestyle, energy |
| **Cafe/Coffee Shop** | coffee shop background, ambient setting | Casual, lifestyle, social |
| **Gym** | gym environment, workout gear, mirrors | Fitness, supplements, athletic |

---

## Setting Selection Rules

1. **Match the product context** — Where would someone actually use this product?
2. **Match the character** — A fitness enthusiast belongs in a gym, not a boardroom
3. **Natural lighting always** — Window light, ambient light. Never studio setups.
4. **Include environmental details** — Fridge hum, coffee steam, gym equipment in background blur
